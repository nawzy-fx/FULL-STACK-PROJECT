Types Of Motors

AC Motor

These motors use alternating current (AC) and are further classified into synchronous and asynchronous (induction) motors. Three-phase AC motors are common in industrial applications, while single-phase AC motors are often found in smaller appliances.

DC Motors

These motors use direct current (DC) and can be brushed or brushless (BLDC). Brushed DC motors are often used in applications requiring a lower cost solution, while brushless DC motors offer higher efficiency and are often found in applications requiring precise control.

MultiPurpose Motors

These motors are designed for specific applications and may have unique features or configurations. Examples include stepper motors, servo motors, an linear motors.
