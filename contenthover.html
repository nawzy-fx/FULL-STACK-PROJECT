Types Of Social Media

Social Networks

These platforms primarily facilitate connecting with others, building relationships, and sharing personal updates. They often include features like profiles, news feeds, messaging, and groups.

Media Sharing Networks

These platforms emphasize sharing visual content like photos and videos. They often include tools for editing, filters, and other creative features.

Discussion Forums

These platforms provide a space for users to engage in conversations, ask questions, share information, and build communities around specific topics.
