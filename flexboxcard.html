Types Of Editing

Developmental Editing

Developmental editing: This type focuses on the overall structure, organization, and content of a piece.

Copy Editing

Copy editing: This stage involves a closer look at the writing, focusing on grammar, punctuation, spelling, and style.

Proofreading

Proofreading: This is the final stage of editing, focusing on catching any remaining errors in spelling, punctuation, and formatting after the content has been finalized.
